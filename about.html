About Our Hotel

"Description about your hotel"

Rocco Forte Hotel is a family-owned hotel that seeks to provide guests with personalized hospitality based on their preferences and is not traditional. Whether guests are looking for a quiet time and plenty of privacy or looking for the most energetic, friendly environment away from home, Rocco Forte Hotel can accommodate. Our goal is to engage our guests and provide them the best possible experience through the most convenient, comfortable, intimate and welcoming environment with harmonious, enterprising and personalized service.

Regardless of the reason for your trip to Cebu (business or pleasure), the main hotels and suites are the lifestyle of your choice - the lifestyle that caters to the demands of high technology or simplicity in hotel accommodation. For our business or leisure guests, the hotel rooms and suites are stylishly furnished and equipped with modern conveniences including telephone, air-conditioned and complimentary WiFi and cable with flat screen television. Some hotel rooms and suites may have integrity bars for the convenience of guests. Major hotels and suites offer luxurious amenities with more than average guest service attended by 24-hour hotel staff.
